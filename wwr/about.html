White Water Rafting

At White Water Rafting, our purpose is to deliver an unforgettable experience that inspires a deep connection with nature and fosters a sense of adventure. Our mission is to provide exceptional service, and safety,while promoting community engagement. As we explore the outdoors we stand by our motto: "Raft, Respect, Repeat."

History

The White Water Rafting company was founded in 1995 by a group of men who sought to conquer the waves. After a series of trials they discovered the secret to rafting success: a combination of excitment and a dash of pure craziness. From humble beginnings on the riverbank, White Water Rafting has grown into an exciting operation, with a team of expert guides who will make you laugh, scream, and possibly even lose your snacks.
